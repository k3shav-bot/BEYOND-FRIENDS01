Beyond Friends is a college project idea for students who want one place to meet new people, find project or hackathon partners, join activities, and share common interests.

Project note

This website is a concept for a student platform. The current goal is to explain the idea clearly and collect real opinions from classmates.

Core idea

One place for different kinds of student connections

Find New Friends

Meet classmates and students with similar interests, clubs, hobbies, and campus vibes.

Activity Partners

Connect with students for campus events, sports, coffee chats, and other shared activities.

Project Partners

Find teammates for assignments, startup ideas, competitions, and college innovation labs.

Hackathon Matching

Search by skills like design, coding, AI, pitching, and product thinking to build the right team.

Game Together

Join gaming circles, campus tournaments, and casual play sessions with other students.

Events And Meetups

Discover study groups, sports, movie nights, and college activities in one place.

How it works

A simple idea for helping students connect better

01

Create a profile with your college, interests, skills, and the type of connections you are looking for.

02

Choose whether you want to find friends, teammates, event partners, or people with similar hobbies.

03

Start conversations, join small groups, and use student feedback to improve the platform idea.

Possible student use cases

  • Finding teammates for class projects
  • Making new friends in college
  • Joining hackathons with the right skill mix
  • Creating study circles and interest groups
  • Meeting people for games and campus activities
